Process

From bug report to root cause in seconds

Four stages, fully automated. No manual log-digging, no context switching between tools.

STEP 01
Ingest

Connect your org's tools. GitMind pulls commits, tickets, threads, and meeting notes into a unified pipeline.

STEP 02
Build

A causal knowledge graph is constructed, linking every line of code to the commit, ticket, and decision behind it.

STEP 03
Analyze

Claude AI traverses the graph backwards from the bug, surfacing the chain of decisions that led to the failure.

STEP 04
Fix

Receive an annotated patch with context: what changed, why, and what future developers need to know.

Capabilities

Debug with full organizational context

GitMind does not just read code. It reads your organization's decision history.

Causal Blame Traversal

Walks backward through commits, tickets, and Slack threads to identify the exact human decision that introduced the bug, not just the line that broke.

Zero Regression Check

Before any patch is approved, GitMind validates all downstream graph nodes. No fix ships that creates a new failure elsewhere.

Annotated Patch Output

Every suggested fix includes three annotations: why the change was made, which original decision it respects, and what future developers must know.
